Unboxed or Irregular size cargo

Please wrap and tape all rolled up goods for the best results. If using bags, please tape tightly to reduce the risk of tearing. Tape and wrap irregular items securely.

Get the volume and weight of your cargo right.

The boxed items are usually priced by weight, as long as they are the same dimensions as our boxes. The volumetric weight of a shipment is when the volume of space is more than the actual weight of the goods. The formula for calculating volumetric weight is Length(L) x Width(W) x Height(H) in CM / 5000. We offer one of the rates for cargo to Nigeria from lodon and other cities across the UK.

Packaging your cargo to Nigeria

Tape boxes securely and fill empty spaces in the box with paper or bubble wrap to prevent movement and damage to the goods during transit. Please wrap and tape irregular items securely.

Labelling your cargo to Nigeria

Tape address label securely to your package. Please use clear tapes, so that the label codes are visible. Do not use loose tags (hanging). Place a duplicate address label inside the box (please place on top and not deep inside the box.)
