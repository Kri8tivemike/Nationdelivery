For a standard saloon car, international car shipping costs to Nigeria (lagos and delivered any where in Nigeria, ) will be on average 1,300.00 for roll on/roll off or 1,400.00 if shipped in our shared container services for saloon cars. However if you are looking to ship either your saloon or 4 x 4 to Nigeria, our vehicle shipping costs start from as little as 850.00 for a saloon or 830 for an SUV
